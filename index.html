To My Perfect Little Angel,

I have so many things running through my mind that I want to tell you, that it is hard to know where to start! First of all I want to say thank you. Thank you for bringing meaning to my life. Thank you for teaching me what it means to truly love someone. Most of all thank you for saving my life.

Since the moment I found out you were coming my life has never been the same. I knew at that moment that nothing I said or did from that point was about me anymore. That thought still terrifies me, and at the same time makes me happier than anything in this world! I have so many hopes and dreams for you. There is not a second that goes by that I don't think about you, and wonder about the little boy and man you will become.

I have so much more to say, but we have all the time in the world. This is just the first of many letters that I can't (and can) wait for you to be big enough to read. I am so proud to be your mother. I love you more than life itself, and want you to know that I will never leave your side.


Love Always,
